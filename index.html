About Me

Hello, I'm Jason, a 3rd-year Computer Engineering student at the University of British Columbia. I enjoy learning how computers work as a whole, from low-level hardware and systems all the way up to high-level software and applications. I hope to get a job in software engineering, building upon the topics I have learned in school.
